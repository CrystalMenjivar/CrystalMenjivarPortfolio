This is a painting of a fish statue that I made in my Graphic Design class Fall of 2014. I used the program SketchBook Pro to paint it. We were to take a picture of anything we wanted and then paint it with 3 different color schemes how ever we wanted. I chose to do analogous colors which is 3 to 4 colors next to each other on the color wheel. I feel that my strongest part of this painting is the bottom of the fish and its shadow. I spent the most time on that part of the painting and am really happy on the outcome of it.

This painting wasn't very hard per se, but it was time consuming. The better I wanted the painting to be the longer and more thorough I had to paint it. This project went under use of resources because we had to use the resource of SketchBook Pro. I really enjoyed doing this project. I always love doing art and testing my art skills. I love watching how my art grows to be better than before.
